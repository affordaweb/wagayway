What We Do

Wagayway Equality Inc. is dedicated to creating a thriving, empowered community for LGBTQIA+ individuals and allies. Our services are designed to foster holistic growth, from health and personal development to advocacy and socio-economic support, ensuring that each member has the resources and confidence to flourish.

Empowerment Through Identity (SOGIE Seminars)

Our SOGIE seminars focus on empowering individuals to embrace their unique identities and celebrate the diversity that strengthens our community. These sessions are more than just educational—they inspire participants to take pride in their journey, encourage authentic self-expression, and build self-confidence. By exploring the power of identity in a supportive space, individuals gain a deeper sense of belonging and are empowered to become advocates for acceptance, both for themselves and for others.

Health Services for Wellness and Support

Prioritizing health is essential to a vibrant life, and our health services offer community-based HIV screening and wellness resources for LGBTQIA+ individuals. In a non-judgmental, supportive environment, we provide free and confidential HIV testing, along with counseling and education on holistic health. Our goal is to promote physical well-being and mental resilience, empowering individuals to make informed choices and prioritize their health confidently, knowing they have our full support.

Rights Education and Advocacy for Change

Rights awareness is a powerful step toward achieving equality, and our rights education initiatives help individuals understand their legal rights and gain the confidence to advocate for change. Through comprehensive workshops and advocacy training, we equip community members with tools to stand up for their rights and foster respect. By building a network of empowered advocates, we're working toward a society that values fairness, justice, and the dignity of every individual.

Socio-Economic Empowerment

Financial stability is a cornerstone of empowerment, and our socio-economic support programs aim to address the unique challenges faced by the LGBTQIA+ community. Through financial literacy workshops, job readiness coaching, and career support, we enable individuals to build economic security and pursue their goals with confidence. By forging partnerships with local businesses and creating opportunities for personal growth, we empower community members to achieve financial independence and contribute to the economy with pride.

Trainings and Development for Personal Growth

Our training and development programs are designed to unlock potential and cultivate the skills needed for personal and professional success. We offer workshops and mentorship that build practical skills, confidence, and leadership. With guidance from experienced mentors, participants are empowered to pursue career growth, overcome barriers, and develop the resilience needed to thrive in any environment.

Support Groups for Community and Connection

Finding community support is essential to well-being, and our support groups provide a compassionate space for individuals to connect, share, and grow. Led by experienced facilitators, these groups encourage open expression, mutual support, and resilience. In sharing their experiences, members strengthen bonds, gain perspective, and support one another in navigating challenges. This network of shared understanding empowers each individual to feel valued and connected.
