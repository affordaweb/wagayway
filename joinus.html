BECOME A VOLUNTEER
Be part of a growing community of advocates. Your passion and time can change lives — join Wagayway Equality as a volunteer today.

WHY JOIN?

As a volunteer at Wagayway Equality, you contribute to meaningful advocacy work for the LGBTQIA+ community. You'll gain hands-on experience, connect with like-minded advocates, and help drive real change in Batangas City and beyond.

WHO CAN JOIN?

We welcome anyone who shares our values of equality, inclusion, and community service — regardless of age, gender identity, or background. All we ask is a willingness to learn and contribute.

HOW TO JOIN

  • Reach out to us via email at wagaywayequalityofficial@gmail.com
  • Follow us on our social media pages and message us directly
  • Visit our ESPASYO Community Center at 3/F Golden Core Ville Bldg., Brgy. Alangilan, Batangas City
  • Attend one of our community events or orientation sessions
