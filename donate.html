Your Impact Matters

Since 2007, Wagayway Equality has been a beacon of hope and support for the LGBTQIA+ community. Your donations help us:

  • Provide free HIV screening and health services
  • Conduct SOGIE seminars and rights education
  • Offer socio-economic empowerment programs
  • Create safe spaces through support groups
  • Develop skills through training programs
  • Advocate for equality and inclusion
